Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

Pinky Shop ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices when using our mobile and web application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and authentication details when you sign in using Firebase Authentication.
  • User content: messages submitted to the chatbot and app-generated responses for feature functionality.
  • Order and profile data: product, cart, order, and profile-related data stored in Cloud Firestore.
  • Technical data: basic device/app diagnostics to improve app reliability and performance.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account securely.
  • To provide shopping, chatbot, and customer support features.
  • To process and manage orders and notifications.
  • To improve app quality, security, and user experience.

3. Authentication and Security

We use Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in and identity management. We apply access controls and security rules to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with trusted service providers required to operate the app (for example, Firebase services), or when required by law.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request account deletion and data removal, subject to legal and operational requirements.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. You can contact us to make a request.

7. Children

Pinky Shop is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post changes on this page and update the effective date.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: pinkyshop.support@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy applies to Pinky Shop mobile and web applications.
